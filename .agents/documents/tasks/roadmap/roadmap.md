# Product Roadmap: Notulensi

This roadmap maps the project backlog and requirements into development phases, focusing on the transition from a local recorder to a comprehensive private meeting assistant.

## Phase 1: Core Infrastructure (Done/In-Progress)
*   **Milestone 1.1**: Flutter setup, Isar (unencrypted), `record`, and System STT.
*   **Milestone 1.2**: Reactive UI for live transcription and waveform display.

## Phase 2: Core Utility & Monetization
*   **Milestone 2.1**: Home screen search and CRUD.
*   **Milestone 2.2**: Regex-based Action Item/Deadline extraction.
*   **Milestone 2.3**: Basic PDF/TXT export and AdMob banner integration.

## Phase 3: Physical Security & Interaction
*   **Milestone 3.1: Physical Safe-Box**: Database encryption + Biometric Auth.
*   **Milestone 3.2: Interaction**: Visual Waveform Markers & Smart Speaker Tagging (VAD).

## Phase 4: Smart Local Intelligence
*   **Milestone 4.1: Privacy**: Local Regex Masking for PII + Audio-to-Calendar bridge.
*   **Milestone 4.2: Ecosystem**: Local Context Linking + Offline Template Engine.

## Phase 5: Productivity & Multitasking
*   **Milestone 5.1**: Noise Suppression (ML) + Local Voice Commands.
*   **Milestone 5.2**: Project Folders + Note Versioning + Insight Dashboard.
*   **Milestone 5.3**: QR-Code Sync + SD-Card Backups + PiP Transcripts.

## Phase 6: Advanced Hardware & Analytics (Vision 2027)
*   **Milestone 6.1: Hardware & Gestures**
    *   **Volume-Key Markers**: Tangible bookmarking during pocket-recording.
    *   **Smartwatch Remote**: Local Bluetooth control for WearOS/Apple Watch.
*   **Milestone 6.2: Multimedia & Storage**
    *   **In-App Meeting Photos**: Timestamp-anchored photos embedded in notes.
    *   **Auto-Silence Trimmer**: Local audio processing to reduce storage size.
*   **Milestone 6.3: High-End Analytics & Rewards**
    *   **Advanced Analytics**: Speech Speed (WPM) tracking & Keyword Heatmaps.
    *   **Reward-Unlocked Content**: AdMob Reward integration for premium templates.
    *   **Local P2P Sync**: WiFi Direct handshake for multi-device transcript merging.
