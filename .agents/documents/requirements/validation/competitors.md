# Competitor Analysis: Offline Meeting Notes

## 1. Comparison Table
| Competitor | Type | Core Features | Pricing | Strengths | Weaknesses |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Otter.ai** | Direct (Cloud) | Real-time STT, AI Summary, Speaker ID. | $10+/mo | Industry-standard accuracy, rich collaboration. | Cloud-only, privacy concerns, high cost. |
| **Fireflies.ai** | Direct (Cloud) | Meeting bot, CRM integration, search. | $10+/mo | Deep integration with Zoom/Teams. | No offline mode, complex for simple use. |
| **Voice Memos** | Indirect (Local) | Simple audio recording. | Free (Native) | 100% offline, zero cost, pre-installed. | No transcription, no summaries, no search. |
| **Obsidian/Notion** | Indirect (Note) | Manual note-taking. | Free/Freemium | Total control, cross-platform sync. | Manual effort, no audio-to-text bridge. |

## 2. Feature Gap Analysis
| Gap | Competitor Shortfall | Opportunity Size |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **100% Offline Privacy** | Cloud tools (Otter/Fireflies) send all audio to servers. | High: Medical, Legal, Corporate Compliance. |
| **Deterministic Summary** | AI tools use probabilistic LLMs (hallucinations possible). | Medium: Users who want "exact" extraction of keywords. |
| **Zero Subscription** | Most STT tools are SaaS only. | High: Users with "subscription fatigue." |

## 3. Pricing Landscape
* **Patterns:** $10-$30/month subscription is standard for cloud transcription.
* **Ranges:** Free (limited) to $240+/year (Pro).

## 4. Strategic Positioning
* **Segment to Avoid:** High-volume enterprise collaboration (Zoom/Teams bots).
* **Gap to Target:** Privacy-first, individual professional recording and instant summarization.
* **Statement:** "The privacy-first alternative to cloud transcription that turns meetings into notes without your data ever leaving your pocket."
