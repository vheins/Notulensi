# Value Proposition: Offline Meeting Notes

## 1. Primary Value Proposition
> For **privacy-conscious professionals** who **need structured meeting notes without cloud risks**, Notulensi **is a 100% offline Flutter application** that **transcribes and automatically structures audio locally**. Unlike **cloud-based AI tools like Otter.ai or Fireflies**, our product **ensures zero data leakage and deterministic, rule-based highlights without requiring an internet connection**.

## 2. Supporting Propositions
* **Total Privacy:** Local on-device STT → Zero bytes sent to cloud → Full data residency and compliance.
* **Instant Structure:** Rule-based parsing → Instant extraction of deadlines and action items → No waiting for "AI processing" delays.
* **Lightweight Speed:** Optimized local storage (Hive/Isar) → <2s startup and instant search → Efficiency on any modern device.

## 3. Elevator Pitch
Notulensi is a 100% offline meeting assistant for professionals who value privacy above all else. It captures audio, provides real-time local transcription, and uses smart deterministic rules to turn messy meetings into actionable notes—all without your data ever leaving your pocket.

## 4. Tagline Options
* **The Outcome:** "Meetings structured, privacy secured."
* **The Problem:** "Notes that never leave your device."
* **The Differentiator:** "100% Offline. 0% Data Leakage."
